# Project 02 - Gender vs Salary (Hypothesis Testing)

Null Hypothesis H0: mean salary (Male) = mean salary (Female)
Alternative H1: mean salary (Male) != mean salary (Female)

Sample sizes: Male=120, Female=110

Descriptive statistics:
| gender   |   count |    mean |     std |     min |     25% |     50% |     75% |     max |
|:---------|--------:|--------:|--------:|--------:|--------:|--------:|--------:|--------:|
| Female   |     110 | 50729.3 | 9065.08 | 33194.6 | 42974.2 | 51794.3 | 56064.5 | 84674.6 |
| Male     |     120 | 51366.6 | 7402.53 | 31042   | 47425   | 51414.2 | 55062.3 | 71705.9 |

T-test result:
t-statistic = 0.5809
p-value = 0.5619

Interpretation:
- If p-value < 0.05, reject H0.
